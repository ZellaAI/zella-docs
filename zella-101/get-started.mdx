## Why Zella?
Zella AI simplifies the integration, management, and optimization of pre-trained AI models
from diverse platforms like OpenAI, Google AI, and Huggingface. Leverage a unified API gateway
to eliminate platform switching complexities. Empower your workflows with dedicated tools for
creating, managing, and versioning prompts. Run data-driven experiments to identify optimal
model-prompt combinations. Reduce AI costs with intelligent caching and prompt optimization
strategies. Unlock the full potential of AI development with Zella's streamlined and cost-effective platform.
